# volleyball-tracker
A simple volleyball tracker app.

## Goal
This application aims to support coaches of youth volleyball in tracking score, playing time, rotational position, and substitions.


## Privacy
All data storage is local to device.


## Try it out
Available here: https://datadrivencoach.github.io/volleyball-tracker/


## About the Author 
Author Cindy Traub is passionate about providing kids of all abilities a positive team experience where they can develop, compete, and celebrate many ways to win.  She has 30+ years of experience as a volleyball player at various levels (high school, college, adult recreational) and over 10 years experience as a youth volleyball coach within CYO (Indianapolis) and CYC (St. Louis), coaching boys and girls volleyball in grades 3-8.  This code was developed in collaboration with generative AI over many iterations and has been game tested.  Additional feedback is always welcome!
